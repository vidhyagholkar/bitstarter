Multiplication, Angles, Ratio, Graphs: fluency through regular practice. A free site for children to practice all aspects of Junior school mathematics (Key Stages 1 and 2 of the UK national curriculum). This won't be a dull passive site with videos. It will be visually appealing, age appropriate and dynamic giving children the motivation to keep coming back for more. Children will receive prizes, awards and certificates of completion. The content will be available on tables, phones and on pc's. Parents will automatically get notified of how their children are doing via email and or text.

Your money will help to fund the preliminary version of this site. State of the art web technologies require expert developers and User Interaction experts. The backend infrastructure will use basic learning algorithms to figure out how hard/easy to make questions and a messaging system will record and transmit to you how are your child is doing. By backing this site you will get immediate access, before the site becomes more publicly and widely available.
